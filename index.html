About This Project

This project ties directly into my interest in environmental engineering - it fetches real environmental data, uses coding skills to visualize the data and creates a practical, shareable tool for tracking sustainability indicators in my community. It's something I plan to keep updating and point to in my college applications. It also aligns with my long-term passion around environmental awareness and provides visibility into environmental indicators of our beautiful city to the community.

About EcoMap
